About The Game

The game is a simple example on the things that happend in Bletchley.

So, there are two levels with two task on every level in the game.

And you have two role for the player. The first one is the codebreaker

who need to guess the coordinates of the other player who is the German.

So, these are the levels:

  • Level 1

    • Task 1:

    The German player input 8 numbers (that are the coordinates for the ship)

    but he can't repeat them. And the codebreaker need to guess at least four

    of them so to break the coordinates and help to his country.

    Task 2:

    The German player input 8 numbers (that are the coordinates for the ship)

    but he CAN repeat them. And the codebreaker need to guess at least four

    of them so to break the coordinates and help to his country.

  • Level 2

    • Task 1:

    You will be the codebreaker and need to guess the coordinates but this time

    the coordinates will be generated from the computer you will be play against

    the computer. Againt 8 numbers and you need to guess 4 of them.

    The numbers can't repeate.

    Task 2:

    You will be the codebreaker and need to guess the coordinates but this time

    the coordinates will be generated from the computer you will be play against

    the computer. Againt 8 numbers and you need to guess 4 of them.

    The numbers CAN repeate.
